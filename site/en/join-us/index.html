Join Us

The Generative Computing Lab at Inha University conducts research on rapidly evolving Generative AI technologies, including Diffusion Models and Large Language Model (LLM) applications. Students interested in joining the lab should email their CV and transcript to Prof. Namhyuk Ahn (nhahn@inha.ac.kr).

At this time, please note that fluency in Korean is a prerequisite for all applicants.
