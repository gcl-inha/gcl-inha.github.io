<b>Generative Computing Lab (GCL)</b> at Inha University aims to advancing Generative AI (Gen AI) that can make a significant impact on our world.
Harnessing the power of Gen AI, we focus on (but are not limited to) the following areas:

        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">

        </li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">

        </li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 10px;">
            <b style="color:#005bac">Ethical Gen AI</b>: We study the societal implications of Gen AI, especially we aim to develop ethical solutions that address and mitigate current concerns regarding the malicious use of this technology.
        </li>
    </ul>
